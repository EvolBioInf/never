Welcome to the Neighbors Server

{{end}} {{define "indexDescr"}}

The Neighbors server gives access to the accessions of genome sequences linked to the NCBI taxonomy.

Starting from one or more target organisms, their genomes are looked up. In addition, the genomes of the targets' closest relatives, their neighbors are looked up. Such pairs of target and neighbor genomes make good starting material for developing diagnostic markers.

The server is based on the Neighbors package and is developed in the Research Group Bioinformatics at the Max-Planck-Institute for Evolutionary Biology in Pl√∂n. For details on marker discovery with Neighbors and related software, please consult our publication.
